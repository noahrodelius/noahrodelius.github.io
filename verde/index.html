WHO/WHAT
The HMS Debate Club will participate in a mini-conference organized and staffed by members of HHS Debate.
WHEN/WHERE
The conference will take place at the Peter J. Biondi Municipal Building in April 2018.
HOW/WHY

Objective: To expose middle schoolers to a novel debate experience outside of regular weekly meetings. Middle schoolers will be given the opportunity to demonstrate speaking skills they have practiced over the course of the year. The conference will also complement the traditional friendly debate with Montgomery.

Structure: Divide seventh graders and eighth graders into two committees with seventh graders having a more basic topic and parliamentary procedure. Workshop sessions for each skill or parliamentary procedure are followed with some committee practice.

ABOUT/CONTACT

This mini-conference was devised by veteran Debate members after a frustratingly fruitless conference. We realized that the key to improving the high school club was dedicated investment in younger talent: paying more attention early on to younger debaters and familiarizing them with the speaking and analytical skills necessary not only to succeed in debate, but also in life.
